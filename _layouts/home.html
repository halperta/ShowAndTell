--- layout: page title: Introduction excerpt: "An undergraduate course at the John Carter Brown Library, Brown University." search_omit: true --- History of the Book in the Americas and Beyond is an undergraduate seminar collaboratively designed and taught by staff at the John Carter Brown Library at Brown University. The instructors are: Neil Safier, director; Hannah Alpert-Abrams, Postdoctoral Fellow in the History of the Book; and Stijn van Rossem, Curator of European Books.
